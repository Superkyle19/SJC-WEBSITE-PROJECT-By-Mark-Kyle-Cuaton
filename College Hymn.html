Saint Joseph College Hymn

Lyrics: Jesus Bacal
Music: Rosario Vivero

Saint Joseph Collge, all hail to thee! We love thee, our Alma Mater,
To thee we give our hearts and all, To thee we dedicate our souls.

To thy teachings now and forever We will be loyal, we’ll be loyal,
To thee we shall live And for thy honor, we shall die.
